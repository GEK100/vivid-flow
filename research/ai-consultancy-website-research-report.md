# AI Consultancy Website Research Report
## Competitive Intelligence for Vivid Flow
**Prepared:** February 2026

---
## 1. Executive Summary
- Market rapidly maturing. Enterprise players set bar high. Boutique UK competitors weak. Clear opportunity.
- UK SME AI: 35-39% using, 11% extensively. Barriers: expertise, cost, ROI uncertainty, privacy.
- Most boutique sites: generic messaging, no case studies, no tools, no social proof.
- Biggest gaps: AI readiness assessments, transparent pricing, live demos, sector pages.
- Vivid Flow: strong foundations, gaps in social proof, form backend, case studies, trust signals.

## 3. What the Best Websites Have in Common

1. Outcome-Led Messaging -- business results not technology in headlines
2. Prominent Social Proof Above the Fold -- client logos or counts in first viewport
3. Structured Case Studies -- Challenge, Solution, Results (quantified), Client quote
4. Clear Methodology -- 3-5 steps with timelines (Vivid Flow has this)
5. Industry-Specific Content -- dedicated pages per vertical
6. Thought Leadership -- minimum 2-4 content pieces per month
7. Multiple Lead Capture -- call booking, gated content, newsletter, interactive tools
8. Team Credibility -- named team with photos, credentials, LinkedIn

---
## 4. Differentiation Opportunities

4.1 Transparent Pricing (Very High) -- Under 5% publish pricing. Huge differentiator for SME buyers.
4.2 AI Readiness Assessment (High) -- 8-12 question quiz. Email-gated. Generates and qualifies leads.
4.3 Live AI Demos (High) -- No boutique competitor has one. Proves capability.
4.4 ROI Calculator (Medium-High) -- Input metrics, get savings estimate.
4.5 Video Content (Medium) -- 60-90 sec explainers, founder story, testimonials.
4.6 Process Transparency (Medium) -- Deliverables, meetings, client time commitment.
4.7 Ethical AI Page (Medium) -- GDPR, data handling, human oversight, bias prevention.

---
## 5. UK SME AI Adoption

- 35-39% of UK businesses using AI (late 2025), only 11% extensively
- 68% of UK SMEs aware but have not implemented meaningful automation
- Adoption lowest in trades, hospitality, agriculture -- Vivid Flow target verticals
- Barriers: expertise (35-55%), cost (30%), ROI uncertainty (25%), privacy (49%), integration (20%)
- UK Government AI Action Plan and grants available to reduce perceived risk
